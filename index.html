The Schaumburg Flying Club is a not-for-profit group of aviation enthusiasts who proudly own and operate two late-model Cessna aircraft. We're based at the centrally located Schaumburg Regional Airport (06C), located just outside of Chicago, IL. The SFC is committed to maintaining a safe fleet of aircraft at reasonable prices. We comply with all ADs and Service Bulletins, and our members perform routine maintenance regularly. And all of our members are fully insured under our club's policy - you won't need to pay for "renter's" insurance. Club members can check aircraft availability and make reservations over the Internet or by telephone in just seconds. We fly for vacation, business, earning advanced ratings, or just a hamburger. We're committed to keeping a safe fleet that's affordable to fly.
